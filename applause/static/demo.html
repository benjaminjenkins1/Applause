Applause

A quick demonstration


At the center of Applause is a tiny (<5 Kb!) script that you put somewhere on each of your articles. The script creates a button on the page, and tracks several metrics. It counts page views, time on page, and referrers, as well as audience approval when users click the clap button.


The process for getting started is simple. Create an account at applauseapp.io and add your domain to your account. Create a key for the domain, then place the generated tag somewhere on your pages. That's it! You can find the data that Applause has collected in your dashboard, which includes reports on views for each page, claps for each page, top referrers, and more.
